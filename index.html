I have also worked on projects related to Recommendation System and Sentiment Analysis.

I have worked as a Full-Stack Software Developer at Intuit India, owning complete user interface module and major business layer components . I Interned at Intuit, Mountain View, California where I was introduced to the world of recommendation system with an enterprise level solution.

With a desire to solve real world data problems, I have worked on several independent projects. Most notable of them include an application of Natural Language Processing into Census Nomenclature, Number Transition and Morphology.
